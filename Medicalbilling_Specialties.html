SPECIALITIES

Billing Gurus can help you take your practice to the next level. 

Allergy & Immunology: Allergy & Immunology billing requires knowledgeable staff that understands the intricacies of this specialty. Our staff assigns exact codes for function testing, spirometry procedures, skin tests and every other treatment rendered. Administering codes related to vaccinations are coded according to age, route of administration and other relevant and applicable specialties. Billing Gurus is able to provide customized solutions to Allergy care centers, immunologists and asthma clinics, through our understanding of this specialty. 

Cardiology : Cardiology billing and coding is a highly complex specialty that requires education and experience to collect all allowable reimbursements in a quick and efficient manner at a low cost to the physician. We understand the practice of cardiology both invasive and non-invasive. Our staff is updated regularly on the rapidly changing procedural and technological advances, coding changes and updated industry guidelines. Cardiology practices face very specific billing and coding challenges. Cardiology billing staff must stay abreast of the rapidly changing procedures and technological advances. Our staff is updated regularly on coding changes and updated industry guidelines.

Dermatology : The volume of patients that dermatologists see on a regular basis far exceeds that of physicians of other specialties. A higher volume does not always translate to higher revenue if the medical billing is not managed properly. Our medical billing professionals understand the complexities of dermatology medical billing to ensure you receive the maximum dollar amount despite the growing trend to consumer driven high-deductible insurance plans. We make sure our medical billing professionals are supplied with ongoing education to ensure accurate and timely reimbursements for your practice.

Internal Medicine : Internal Medicine is the Medical Specialty dealing with the Prevention, Diagnosis and Treatment of Adult Diseases. We have more than 10 years of experience in Internal Medicine Billing, Our coders and Medical Billing experts have rich experience in internal medicine and all its sub specialties billing. We are providing medical billing services to leading physician groups in USA.

Emergency Medicine : We understand how quickly Emergency Medicine is changing leaving emergency medicine professionals with growing challenges such as regulatory requirements, diligent compliance enforcement and decreased reimbursements. Our medical billing professionals understand the complexities ER physicians are faced with such as emergency room overcrowding and staffing shortages. We make sure our medical billing professionals are supplied with ongoing education to ensure accurate and timely reimbursements.

Gastroenterology : Billing for gastroenterology is challenging and requires a medical billing staff that has extensive knowledge specific to this specialty. Inexperienced medical billing firms working with gastroenterology specialists make costly mistakes that lead to denials and underpayments for incorrect services or procedures. Knowledge of the specific documentation requirements needed for gastroenterological procedures such as colonoscopy are a must. These details, when overlooked add up to lower levels of reimbursements which impact the bottom line significantly.
